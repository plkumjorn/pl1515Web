Hello, my name is "Piyawat". I'm Thai. Some of my international friends call me "Peter", while my Thai friends call me "Tui"/"ตุ้ย". (Please feel free to choose the one you prefer.) Currently, I'm a PhD student in Computing, under the supervision of Prof Yike Guo, at Data Science Institute of Imperial College London. My research interests include Inter-disciplinary Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Semantic Web, and Social Network Analysis.
