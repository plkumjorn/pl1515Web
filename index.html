Hello, my name is "Piyawat". I'm Thai. Some of my international friends call me "Peter", while my Thai friends call me "Tui"/"ตุ้ย". (Please feel free to choose the one you prefer.) Currently, I'm a PhD student in Computing, under the supervision of Prof Francesca Toni and Prof Lucia Specia at Imperial College London.

Research Interests:
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) & Explainability in Deep NLP
  • Knowledge Graph & Semantic Web
  • Machine Argumentation & Argument Mining
  • Inter-disciplinary Data Mining
